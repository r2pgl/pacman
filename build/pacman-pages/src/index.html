HOW TO PLAY: Use keys to move pacman. Ghosts try to chase player and on collision player looses a life.

Player starts with 5 lives and can have upto 7. Collect to increase lives. Use to gain ability to destroy ghosts, ability lasts for 10 seconds & ghosts try to runaway from player. Eating a ghost gives bonus of 200 points.
